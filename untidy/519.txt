### States by Size in Square Miles

U.S. states listed in order by size in square miles of land area - Alaska to
Rhode Island. The land area of the entire United States is 3,531,905 square
miles.

Alaska is by far the biggest state - you could fit Rhode Island (the smallest
state) into Alaska several hundred times, yet Rhode Island has a significantly
larger population according to the U.S. Census Bureau's estimated population
figures for 2017, with Alaska at 739,795 compared to Rhode Island's 1,059,639
people.
