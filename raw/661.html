Question 466647: ] The formula for degrees Celsius is: C = 5\9 (F 32), where F stands for degrees Fahrenheit.
Part 1: Solve the formula for F. Show all steps.
Part 2: Determine how many degrees Fahrenheit 6 degrees Celsius is.

Part 1: Solve the formula for F. Show all steps.
C = 5/9 (F 32)
9C = 5(F - 32)
F - 32 = 9C/5
F = 9C/5 + 32
--------------
Part 2: Determine how many degrees Fahrenheit 6 degrees Celsius is.
F = 9*6/5 + 32
F = 54/5 + 32
F = 10.8 + 32
F = 42.8
-----------------------
