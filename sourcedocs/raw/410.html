Carbon dioxide is one of the most abundant gasses in the atmosphere. Carbon dioxide plays an important part in vital plant and animal process, such as photosynthesis and respiration.

These processes will be briefly explained here.Green plants convert carbon dioxide and water into food compounds, such as glucose, and oxygen.

This process is called photosynthesis.
